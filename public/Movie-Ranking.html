Top 3 Movies of All the Time

The Stranger Things.

Stranger Things is a Netflix series that combines elements of horror, mystery, and nostalgia. The show follows a group of teenagers in the 1980s who investigate a mysterious portal that releases hostile supernatural creatures into their town.
It has Drama
It has Fantasy & Horror
It has so much of good characters

Loockwood & Co.

Lockwood & Co is a thriller series for readers 10+ about a group of young agents who form a psychic investigation agency to hunt down and destroy murderous ghosts who are terrorising London. The show is based on a collection of books by Jonathan Stroud.

It is a Crime Thriller
It has fantasy and Drama.

Asur

A psychopath killer with a twisted philosophy deep-seated in Indian mythology is on the loose. Two forensic officers from the CBI are close on heals to catch the serial killer but with a twist of fate, they get caught in a dreadful situation. How will this end? Will the real face of this ASUR be revealed?

It has mythology
Its has Suspense
And its has Cyber warfare
